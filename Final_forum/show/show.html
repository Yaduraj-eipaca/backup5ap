"Tamaso ma Jyotirgamaya"

This site is a free platform to connect school students with volunteer subject experts. This platform aims to use the power of the internet & technology to give support to needy students from all sections of society, irrespective of their social/economical status. Volunteer subjects experts have volunteered to help needy students free of cost. Any student who wants to use this platform can ask his questions after creating a free account on this site. These questions will be answered by the subject experts at the possible earliest for them. Students can also request dedicated one to one interactive sessions with the subject experts, in the free time slots indicated by the subject experts. of the different subjects of their school Curriculum. For more information on how to use this platform as a student or to join this platform as an expert, please refer to the “Help” section.
